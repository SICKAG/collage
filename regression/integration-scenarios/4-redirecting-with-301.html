Composite frontends may often choose to redirect to their actual fragment urls from a url on their origin server.

This way arrangements can have easier control over the sources of their fragments.

Since actual frontends need to load even more resources, this is often done not via a serverside rewrite, but with a 301 redirect

In our case, our fragment is configured to be at /redirect/... which will then redirect (301) to the real server at port 8081
